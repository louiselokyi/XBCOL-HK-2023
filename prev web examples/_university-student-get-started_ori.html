THE 2022 HONG KONG BLOCKCHAIN OLYMPIAD FOR UNIVERSITY STUDENTS

Timeline

  • Jan 4 - ideation sessions start - see below for details
  • Jan 28 - register school (third parties may also nominate schools/teachers)
  • Feb 28 - register teams - see below for details
  • May 15 - ideation sessions end
  • April 26 noon - whitepapers due
  • June 13 noon - HKBCOL finals materials due
  • June 19 SATURDAY ALL DAY - HKBCOL Finals

BLOCKCHAIN TRAINING

  • Self-paced online at training.ibcol.org
  • Content selection include BC 1xx, BC 2xx, etc
  • Live coding workshops BC 3xx every Saturday (2-5pm) @ Kowloon Tong (either CityU or Innocentre)
  • Tutorials for BC 10x in January Saturdays 1-2pm
  • Tutorials for BC 20x in February Saturdays 1-2pm
  • Tutorials for BC 2nx in March Saturdays 1-2pm
  • Jan-Mar first Friday of each month (Apr, 2nd Fri) 4-7pm: writing workshop + HSBC mentoring
  • June 5, 12 Saturday 2-5pm - pitching & interview workshop

IDEATION SESSIONS

  • Jan-Apr: Mon (4-7pm), Thu (4-7pm) @ Kowloon Tong (either CityU or Innocentre)
  • Students bring ideas and split up by topics into breakout rooms (1-2 mentors in each)
  • Topics: FinTech, EduTech, RegTech, GovTech, IoTech, MedTech, PropTech, LogTech, SciTech, Health 3.0 Industrie 4.0, Society 5.0

Submissions

  • Whitepaper, 1 to 10 pages, style guide must be followed, unlimited pages of references and appendices — PDF format
  • Posterboard, 36x48-inches at 300 dpi, no limitation on style — PDF format or Figma link
  • Pitch deck, no limitation on pages, style, etc — PDF format
  • 1-min elevator pitch, any or all teammates may talk, but keep this clear and concise — youtube link or MP4
  • 10-min presentation pitch, with pitch deck — youtube link or MP4
