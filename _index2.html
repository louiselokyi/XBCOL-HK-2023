Pitch


@ 9:30am – 1pm

In the morning of Saturday 20 June, finalists of the Hong Kong Blockchain Olympiad shall present their projects to judges and members of the public through the medium of a 10-minute pitch presentation. Projects shall be evaluated based on commercial (viability) and technical (feasibility) merit. The audience is encouraged to observe, in consideration for the People's Choice Awards.

Expo


@ 2pm – 5:30pm

In the afternoon of Saturday 20 June, finalists of the Hong Kong Blockchain Olympiad shall present their projects to judges and members of the public through the medium of posterboards in an exhibition. Projects shall be evaluated based on commercial (viability) and technical (feasibility) merit. The audience is encouraged to observe, in consideration for the People's Choice Awards.
