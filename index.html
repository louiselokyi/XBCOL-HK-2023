Welcome to the 6th Hong Kong Blockchain Olympiad
Bring Hong Kong into the Web3 Age

Competition Synopsis

Learn & practice hard skills demanded by the current market.

Design legal & compliant blockchain-enabled solutions.

Earn a chance to represent Hong Kong at the International Blockchain Olympiad finals in Amsterdam, Netherlands, EU.

HKBCOL invites teams of 1 to 6:

  • Current students studying at secondary or tertiary institutions or;
  • Recent student within 3 years of hiatus or graduation.

Why Join?

  • Future proof Skills for Self and Society
    1. Blockchain technology is a rapidly evolving field with the potential to revolutionize various industries and applications. As students, you have the opportunity to help shape this exciting and dynamic industry. In this competition, you can future-proof your skills and gain valuable experience in a field that is poised to grow rapidly in the coming years.
    2. Participating in this challenge is a great way to showcase your knowledge and abilities, and network with industry experts and professionals. Additionally, these events can help you stay up-to-date with the latest developments and best practices in the industry, and gain exposure to a wide range of blockchain projects and applications. So, if you're passionate about blockchain technology, don't hesitate to get involved and start making your mark in this exciting and innovative field!
  • Certifications
    1. 20 teams will be shortlisted to proceed to the final round of competition hosted in Amsterdam. Top 3 will be presented with Gold, Silver and Bronze Medals. Distinction awards will be given to those who demonstrate exceptional knowledge and skills in blockchain technology, and merit awards will be given to those who show promise and potential in the field.
    2. If you have an idea, participating in the competition is a great way to present and exchange ideas, and potentially gaining recognition for your work!

Workshops

To support and prepare potential participants of IBCOL, there will be online courses running through April to November 2023. The courses will be delivered in two phases: beginner (April - July 2023) and advanced level (August - November 2023). Stay tuned!

Sponsors and Supporting Organisations

IBCOL offers a unique opportunity for students to showcase their skills and innovative ideas in the field of blockchain technology. IBCOL partners with academic institutions to provide access to facilities, resources, and expertise to help participants develop and refine their ideas. IBCOL also partners with companies to access funding, mentorship, and real-world insights to help participants turn their ideas into viable products or services. By working together, these partners have helped to support IBCOL to drive growth and promote the wider adoption of blockchain technology at international level.
